Fluffy Cheese Muffin

Description

Ingredients 12 servings 100 g Cream cheese 160 ml fresh cream 80 g pancake mix 69 g granulated sugar 2 eggs 1 tbs lemon juice melted butter

Cooking

1

Mix cream cheese, freah cream, yolks and lemon Whisk egg white with sugar to dirm consistency

2

Fold and combine above two in two steps Coat muffin tray with butter.

3

Pour the mix in tray. And put in oven. 160C for 30 min and 180C for 10min.
